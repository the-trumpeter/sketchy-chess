### There are no rules...
A silly little app I made on a coach in the middle of nowhere, coincidentally on 1st April, so that I could play chess with my friend in the seat behind me (my 13" Macbook didn't fit on the windowsill very well).\
There's no move validation, no turn taking... it's every piece for itself.
